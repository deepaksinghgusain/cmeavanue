I can’t find my CPE Certificate. What should I do?
Every learner has access to their learners portal, via our website, where certificates are kept for ease of access. Please log into the learners portal with the details that were provided to you upon registration. If you are not able to find your login details, you can reset your password. We also email certificates to all learners after each course. Check your inbox (incl. spam folder). If something seems amiss, and neither options are available, please email us at "cpe@cpewarehouse.com"

Our Contribution
We donate $1 to the Alzheimer’s Foundation of America with every purchase you make. • Alzheimer's Disease is the 6th leading cause of death in the U.S., and we’re dedicated to supporting breakthroughs in its treatment.
Alzheimer’s Financial and Health Impact
In 2019, Alzheimer’s cost the U.S. $290 billion, projected to rise to $1.1 trillion by 2050. • Currently, 5.8 million Americans are living with Alzheimer’s, a number expected to nearly triple by 2050.
Our Ongoing Commitment
Apart from serving our customers, we aim to contribute to the next big breakthrough in Alzheimer’s treatment. • Every purchase helps fund research that will bring us closer to a cure
